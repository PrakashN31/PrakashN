About

I am a field biologist and researcher with hands-on experience spanning vegetation surveys, long-term ecological monitoring, GIS-based biodiversity assessments, and socio-ecological fieldwork across Karnataka and Andhra Pradesh. My work spans from the Western Ghats to coffee agroforestry landscapes, with a growing interest in plant-pollinator network dynamics, thermal tolerance of tropical trees, and evidence-based conservation. I am comfortable leading field teams, collaborating across disciplines, and translating raw ecological data into actionable restoration strategies.
